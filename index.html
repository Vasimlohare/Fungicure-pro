Fungal Infection Information

Understand your condition better

🦠 Types

Ringworm, Athlete's Foot, Jock Itch, Nail Fungus, Candida — all treatable with our Ayurvedic formula.

⚠️ Symptoms

Itching, redness, circular rash, scaling skin, burning sensation and discoloration.

🔍 Causes

Sweat, humidity, shared towels/clothing, weakened immunity, poor hygiene.

🛡️ Prevention

Keep skin dry, wear clean clothes, avoid sharing personal items, maintain hygiene.

💊 Treatment

Apply consistently. Full course is essential for complete recovery.
